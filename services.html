Service FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity for bulk materials?

Minimum bulk order is 10 units or 500kg weight depending on the material. Timber comes in pallet minimums of 20 boards, while cement sacks start from 50-bag pallets.

Can I extend a tool or equipment rental period?

Yes. Call the rental desk before your return deadline to extend up to 7 additional days at the same daily rate. Extended beyond 7 days will receive a 10% weekly discount applied.

Is the engineering consultation included in a contract account?

Pro and Enterprise accounts receive 3 free consultation hours per month. Additional sessions are billed at $95/hour with a minimum 2-hour block booking per visit.

How accurate is the computer paint color matching?

Our Spectrophotometer achieves Delta-E accuracy of 0.05 or lower, which is imperceptible to the human eye. We guarantee a free re-mix within 30 days if there is any visible variation.
