Toolix Heritage
& Commitment

Founded in 2001, Toolix started as a local hardware storefront supplying structural nails and basic hand tools. Over two decades of customer-centric commitment helped us expand into the region's largest independent hardware provider.

Today, our 50,000 sq ft showroom warehouse houses everything from professional computerized paint mixing terminals, heavy duty equipment rentals, and specialized electrical and plumbing fittings. We make building easy.
