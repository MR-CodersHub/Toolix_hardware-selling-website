Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Toolix deliver to construction sites?

Yes, we operate a fleet of flatbed crane transit trucks to deliver bulk raw lumber, concrete batches, and heavy machinery directly to commercial and home project coordinates.

How do I apply for a commercial contractor line of credit?

You can submit an application via our contractor portal online or in-showroom. Licensed contracting firms are eligible for up to a $50,000 credit limit with 30-day interest-free payment terms.

Can you process exact custom paint color matching?

Absolutely. Bring any paint scrap or color swatch to our paint counter, and our computerized Spectrophotometer device will analyze and mix the identical tone in weatherproof paint in under five minutes.

What is your return and tools warranty policy?

We provide a 30-day return policy for unused products in matching retail cartons. Hand tools feature a Toolix lifetime warrantee, while power tools carry manufacturer warranties from Bosch, DeWalt, and Makita.
